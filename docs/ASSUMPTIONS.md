# Assumptions

This proposal site is directional. It is built from the CPS source decks for the
relationship management programmes, and it is meant to start a conversation, not
to serve as a signed scope of work. A few things are worth being explicit about.

## No internal CalBank data

- We do not have access to CalBank's internal performance, portfolio, or customer
  data. Nothing on the site is drawn from CalBank systems.
- Statements about the challenge and the opportunity are framed from CPS market
  experience and the source decks, not from a CalBank diagnostic.
- Where the site references segments, targets, or outcomes, treat them as the
  shape of the work, to be confirmed with CalBank.

## Directional strategy only

- The programme structure, the four transitions, the journeys, and the pricing
  come straight from the CPS decks for RM for SME Bankers (Express) and RM for
  Business, Commercial and Corporate.
- Pricing is per delegate as quoted in the decks (R 17 840 and R 17 270 per
  banker, R 2 510 per line manager, minimum cohort of 20). Final pricing depends
  on cohort sizes, segments, and the integration agreed with CalBank.
- Cadence details (contact sessions and online learning per transition) are
  summarised from the decks and will be finalised in delivery planning.

## Requires validation post-approval

- Banking integration, segment focus, and success measures are co-created with
  CalBank after approval.
- The Category B positioning on the skills development matrix is subject to
  endorsement from CalBank's verification agencies.
- Any CalBank logo, brand colours, or co-branding would need to be supplied and
  approved by CalBank before use. The current site uses a plain text "Prepared
  for CalBank" mark only.

## Content provenance

- Copy is rewritten in the CPS voice (second person, sentence case, South African
  spelling, specific and credentialed, no em dashes) from three source files:
  the SME Bankers deck, the BCC deck, and the Client Value Impact front line deck
  (used for structure and the four transitions).
- Imagery, accreditation badges, and client logos are reused from the CPS Design
  System asset set.
